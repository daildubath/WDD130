If you've ever done a "powerpoint night", you know what this is:

Does it make sense? No, but it leaves it open to the interpretation of the presenter. I originally had it a game: If someone in the audiance laughed or smiled, they were out. If everone laughed but one, that person one. They were aloud to ask questions as long as they were in. Last, if there were two or more audience members are in, the presenter wins. This forces the presenter to be serious, while the audience members try to crack eachother up and ask dumb questions. Great idea, right? Well, it didn't work at all. So don't try it. They ended up skipping the last 3 slides because they got board.
